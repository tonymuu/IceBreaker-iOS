Hey.

IceBreaker is a iOS app that helps you start engaging conversation with people you would absolutely love to meet. Developed by Tony Mu, built on Swift, and it is open source right now!

More

Make Friends

And maybe one friend becomes someone more than a friend. IceBreaker helps you engage in natural and fun conversations. Showing you three interests a poetential friend has, you can start conversations in a most comfortable way.

Grasp the Moment

90% of the people you saw today are 100% of the people you will never see again. IceBreaker encourages you to socialize and have fun with others right here, right now. With IceBreaker, you become more confident and more attractive. You make potential friends real friends.

People Nearby

No more fake people like other social app. No more messaging a stranger behind a screen. Know who you are talking to, and start talking to them! IceBreaker only shows real people around you who are also using IceBreaker. Start breaking ice, start making friends!
